Why Choose Us

High-Speed Internet

Enjoy seamless connectivity with our high-speed fiber internet for work and entertainment.

Delicious Meals

Guests are served three wholesome meals a day, along with evening tea and snacks to keep you refreshed. We include fish on most days, and chicken is served every Wednesday. To keep things exciting, we also surprise our guests with special dishes once a month.

Resident Responsibility

While we ensure regular cleaning of common areas, residents are kindly expected to maintain cleanliness in their individual rooms. A security deposit of ₹2000 is collected at the time of admission. It is refundable at the time of move-out.

Location and Accesibility

Conveniently located near CyberPark, Hilite Business Park, and Hilite Mall, everything you need is within easy reach. Whether it’s shopping, dining, or work, everything is just a stone’s throw away, making it the perfect spot for students and working professionals alike.

Laundery Access

Residents are provided with a separate space and stone slab for self-service laundry. Washing is to be done by the guests themselves.

Activities

We believe in building a vibrant community and also make sure every occasion feels special. Our residents come together for festive meals, movie nights, and other fun gatherings—creating memories that last. Whether it’s a celebration or simply a casual hangout, there’s always something to bring us closer.
